Timeline Of Sachin's Carrer

  • 1973
    Birth
    Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar is born on April 24 in Mumbai, India.
  • 1988
    First-class debut
    Tendulkar makes his first-class debut for Mumbai, scoring 100 runs in his first innings.
  • 1989
    Test debut
    Tendulkar makes his Test debut for India against Pakistan at the age of 16 headOfCards and 205 days, becoming the youngest player to do so.
  • 1990
    ODI debut
    Tendulkar makes his ODI debut for India against Pakistan, scoring 54 runs.
  • 1992
    World Cup
    Tendulkar scores 523 runs in the 1992 World Cup, including a century against England.
  • 1994
    Arjuna Award
    Arjuna Award Tendulkar is awarded the Arjuna Award, India's highest sporting honor for cricketers.
  • 1996
    World Cup
    Tendulkar scores 45 runs in the 1996 World Cup final, as India loses to Sri Lanka.
  • 1998
    Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award
    Tendulkar is awarded the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award, India's highest sporting honor.
  • 1999
    Married
    Tendulkar marries Anjali Mehta.
  • 2003
    World Cup
    Tendulkar scores 673 runs in the 2003 World Cup, including two centuries. .
  • 2004
    ICC World XI
    Tendulkar is named in the ICC World XI for the first time.
  • 2008
    Padma Vibhushan
    Tendulkar is awarded the Padma Vibhushan, India's second-highest civilian honor.
  • 2010
    100th century
    Tendulkar scores his 100th international century, becoming the first batsman to do so.
  • 2013
    Retirement
    Tendulkar retires from international cricket.

Qualities I Admire About Sachin Tendulakar

  • Consistent
    Sachin Tendulkar showed remarkable consistency throughout his cricketing career, scoring runs consistently in various formats of the game.
  • Skillful
    His batting skills were exceptional, displaying perfect timing and a wide array of shots.
  • Perseverance
    Sachin's determination and perseverance helped him overcome challenges and bounce back from setbacks.
  • Humble
    Despite achieving great success, he remained humble and down-to-earth, earning respect from fans and fellow cricketers.
  • Sportsmanship
    He exemplified sportsmanship by respecting opponents and promoting fair play.
  • Longevity
    Sachin's ability to maintain peak performance over an extended period demonstrated his dedication to the sport.
